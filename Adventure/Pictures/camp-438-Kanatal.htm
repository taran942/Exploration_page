When to Visit

The best time you can think of going to Kanatal is actually anytime! The pleasant weather being an asset to Kanatal, it welcomes its visitors with the same all through the year.

Summer (March – June): During this time, the weather in Kanatal remains pleasant as maximum temperatures sore up to a maximum of 28 degrees Celsius.

Winter (October – February): The magic of Kanatal winters gives one an enchanting feel as the entire vicinity is enveloped in white snow, giving it the appearance of a winter-land ( that the legendary Alice-could dream of while she was in her wonderland!) The temperature during this time of the year plummets down to as low as 1 degree Celsius.

Monsoon (July – September): The light to heavy monsoon rain showers again invite you to witness Kanatal valley flourish and become alive.
